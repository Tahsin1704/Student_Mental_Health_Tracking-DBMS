# 🧠 Student Mental Health Tracking System

---

## 📖 Project Overview

**Student Mental Health Tracking System** is a database management project developed to efficiently manage and organize student mental health records. The system stores and maintains information related to students, mental health surveys, therapy sessions, diagnoses, treatments, medications, psychological assessments, observations, support groups, attendance, and emergency interventions. It provides a centralized relational database for managing mental health services and monitoring student well-being through structured data management.

### 🔹 Project Summary

- Centralized student mental health database
- Student profile and medical record management
- Mental health survey management
- Therapy session management
- Diagnosis and treatment tracking
- Medication record management
- Psychological assessment management
- Student observation recording
- Mental health support group management
- Attendance tracking
- Emergency intervention management
- SQL-based relational database with entity relationships

# ✨ Key Features

- Stores and manages student mental health records
- Maintains therapy session and therapist information
- Records diagnoses, treatments, and medications
- Manages psychological assessments and observations
- Tracks mental health surveys and support group activities
- Maintains attendance and emergency intervention records
- Enforces data integrity using primary and foreign keys
- Supports efficient data retrieval through SQL queries
